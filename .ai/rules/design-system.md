# Design System Tokens

## Color Palette
- `--color-primary`: `#197fe6` (Brand Main Blue)
- `--color-secondary`: `#6c757d` (Muted Gray)
- `--bg-light`: `#f8f9fa` (Main Background)

## Typography
- `--font-main`: `'Pretendard', sans-serif`
- **Base Font Weight**: 400 (Regular), 700 (Bold)

## Shapes & Shadows
- `--radius-lg`: `12px` (Card, Button, Modal)
- `--shadow-sm`: `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)` (Subtle depth)

## Components Styling
- **Navbar**: White background with shadow, Sticky-top
- **Cards**: Borderless, 12px radius, subtle shadow
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners (12px), primary color focus
